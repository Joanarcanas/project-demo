Pet Adoption

Pet adoption is taking a pet home in order to provide shelter for them. Just like humans, pet have the right to a better home, medical care, food and love. Our aim is to provide homeless pets in particular cats a home to stay in. A home and owners where they will feel loved and treasured. To protect animals is to protect life. This reduces the number of stray pets in the streets.
